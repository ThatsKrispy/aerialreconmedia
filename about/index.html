Who We Are

Aerial Recon Media is the premier, full-service Photography, Videography & Inspection Drone Service company based out of Miami, FL. We offer an impressive array of media services for the Real Estate, Architecture, Engineering, and Construction Industry.

We pride ourselves in the quality of our work, fast turnaround, and customer satisfaction. At Aerial Recon Media, our team becomes part of your office!

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

We are committed to providing exceptional customer service, building strong business relationships as a trustworthy partner by contributing to our clients' businesses' success.

We provide high-quality media content in a creative format that will enhance the perceived value of our clients' marketing goals and needs.

We never stop learning so that we can find solutions for our clients' needs and our own. We honestly conduct business, ensuring that all parties benefit from our business dealings.

We will build customer loyalty by nurturing our client relationships. Getting involved with the community and becoming a presence in our industry. We are determined to succeed.

We are a passionate and capable team who can develop any creative requirement requested by our clients. We enjoy every aspect of our life, and we work with the best disposition, energy, and professionalism.

Our team is made up of Directors, Editors, Post-Producers, Sound Producers, FAA Licensed Drone Pilots/Operators, and all the technical personnel to materialize any visual idea you have in mind.

We have faith in our abilities to grow not only our business but to grow personally as well. We challenge ourselves in every project to exceed your expectations.
